Lasgana

Description

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onions and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasgana noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

Steps

  • Make the meat sauce.
  • Cook the noodles.
  • Make the ricotta mixture.
  • Layer the lasagna
  • Cover with foil and bake
  • Let the lasagna rest before serving
